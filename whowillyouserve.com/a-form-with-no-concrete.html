A form with no concrete?

Ever tried to build a house on a formed concrete pad with no concrete in it?

What?

Imagine this: You save up for years (or get a good line of credit) and finally start to build your dream home. The concrete workers come out and build the form for your house pad. It needs to be filled with concrete, and then you can build on the foundation the workers poured.
But wait! Before the concrete workers pour the concrete, the boss comes over to you and says, "Well there you are: its done now -you can build your house..." Then they pack up their tools and hit the road, leaving you with a form of a foundation with no concrete in it.

Absurd you say?

Of course. Who would ever think of such a thing. But in the spiritual realm this happens all the time. Many people try to build their house on a empty form. There is a verse from the Bible that speaks of this:

"Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away."
